About

From sound to screens, I design systems that keep people in flow.

I started in commercial music and audio production. Years inside Logic Pro, Ableton Live, and Pro Tools taught me how easily momentum breaks when tools add friction. That experience taught me to design with focus in mind: reduce cognitive load, clarify decisions, and keep the next step obvious.

In parallel, I teach piano, voice, and flute to students with different learning styles. Teaching reinforced the same principle: powerful tools can be deep, but they should still feel clear, teachable, and accessible.

Now I apply that mindset to complex digital products, especially creative software, music tech, and learning platforms, where thoughtful systems design can support both mastery and confidence.
