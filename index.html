Process

How I built this portfolio

I started in Figma, where I designed the layouts and built the design system first. Then I exported the design tokens and used Claude to support both design refinement and code implementation, which helped me keep the system more consistent while speeding up iteration. I chose this flow because it gave me a strong design foundation first, while still letting AI support both the creative and development side of the process. After that, I uploaded the project to GitHub and deployed it with Vercel.

About

From sound to screens.

With a background in creative audio for multimedia and interactive art, I've always been interested in how different elements come together to shape an experience. I moved into digital product design to apply that same creativity in a more practical, everyday context.

My work is grounded in systems thinking, with a focus on making experiences efficient, simple, and meaningful.
