UX/UI Design Mobile Provider Experience Marketplace

TiriVelo is a pet-care marketplace connecting pet owners with local care providers.

It had an existing desktop provider experience, and I designed the mobile provider app to support on-the-go service work, align shared flows, and create a scalable foundation.

Problem

Unclear fit for how providers actually work.

User research showed that providers needed a platform that could support the business they already had. But early concepts made key tasks like earnings, scheduling, and follow-up feel unclear.

Problems that stood out

  1. Earnings lacked clarity — payout details were hard to understand

  2. Scheduling felt unclear — next steps were not always obvious

  3. Follow-up felt fragmented — repetitive screens added unnecessary complexity

Context & Constraints

  1. Changing requirements — scope shifted often in a fast-moving startup

  2. Unclear ownership — team responsibilities were not always defined

  3. Limited provider input — direct feedback was hard to get early on

  4. Tight timeline — required focusing only on highest-impact areas

Role

Product Designer

Platform

iOS Mobile App

Timeline

3 weeks

Methods

  • Rapid interviews
  • Experience mapping
  • Workflow analysis
  • Focused on improving trust-critical workflows (earnings & scheduling)

Focus

Provider Trust Earnings Clarity Scheduling Control

Strategy

To address the main challenges, I focused on key areas that would create the most impact.

Scope & Risk

Narrowed the work to Earnings and Edit Schedule to keep the project focused and reduce overlap across teams.

Trust Focus

Concentrated on the moments that most affect provider trust: understanding earnings, managing availability, and knowing what happens after a service ends.

Consolidate & Hierarchy

Reduced repetition and clarified information so important details were easier to find and understand.

Validate & Map

Grounded decisions in provider conversations and journey mapping to identify gaps and reduce guesswork.

Solution

Improving Scheduling Clarity and Control

This update made scheduling easier to understand and manage by helping providers quickly read availability, make changes without losing context, and set more precise time ranges.

Feature 01

Availability at a Glance

Providers can quickly see which days and times are open or booked using clear visual cues in the calendar.

Feature 02

In-Context Editing

Providers can update availability without leaving the calendar. The schedule stays visible while an edit panel slides up, so they can make changes without losing context.

Feature 03

Precision Time Control

Providers can adjust time in smaller increments using a familiar picker, similar to the Apple Clock, making it easier to set accurate schedules.

Making Earnings Easier to Understand

I redesigned the earnings view to make payouts easier to review at a glance. Instead of showing only a final number, the screen gives providers a clearer picture of how their earnings are calculated.

  1. Breakdown instead of total only — shows the parts behind the payout, rather than only one final amount

  2. Tips visibility — makes tips easy to see as part of total earnings

  3. Clear payout structure — separates fees and final earnings so providers can quickly understand what they take home

Reducing Redundancy in Post-Service Flows

I streamlined the post-service flow by merging overlapping screens, adding missing context, and making follow-up easier to review.

Feature 01

Unified Booking Summary

Combined duplicate post-service screens into one clearer summary, so providers could review booking details, visit information, and earnings in one place.

Feature 02

Added Owner & Pet Context

Made owner and pet details easier to access within the flow, giving providers clearer context around who the service was for.

Feature 03

Clear and Safe Feedback Flow

The original flow lacked context and repeated feedback information across screens. I created a reusable feedback component tied to the booking, making follow-up clearer and more consistent across the product.

Results

Providers reported feeling in control for the first time.

Providers felt more confident navigating the platform. By improving clarity across key workflows, the experience became easier to review, easier to act on, and easier to trust.

50%

Fewer accessibility issues after improving readability, contrast, and interaction clarity

45%

Fewer extra steps and faster review of key information

30%

Less friction in understanding earnings through a clearer payout breakdown

"I need to know right away what needs my attention."

This insight highlighted how much providers needed clearer signals, stronger structure, and more reliable follow-up.

Trust came from clarity. Better visibility into earnings, scheduling, and post-service details helped the platform feel more dependable without adding complexity.
